We are happy to present Kingdom of Krawall, a fast-paced turn-based 4X game where you try to conquer your enemies.

Become a King

You are the king, and your decisions will decide the fate of your little kingdom.

Cards Are Your Orders

Cards represent your orders. Depending on where you use them, they have different effects. Servants, for example, can be used to claim neutral land, gather resources on your own tiles, be trained into units in some buildings, or craft items in others.

Conquer Them All

To be victorious, read the map, expand your realm, exploit resources and anomalies, and claim important positions before your rivals do to finally conquer their palaces.

Construct Buildings

Builders construct buildings. Buildings produce resources and cards, and give you training and crafting options. The tile decides which building options you have.

Grow Your Economy

Each turn, your kingdom produces cards and resources. A strong economy is the key to a powerful army. Buildings and servants have adjacency bonuses, and items can boost both directly.

Anomalies and Special Buildings

Anomalies allow the construction of special buildings. Some of them give you cards, while others provide training options or resources. Claiming them will give you an advantage and heavily influence the course of the game.

Equip Your Units

Items can be used to boost buildings or power up your units. The blacksmith can craft armor, which gives your units more combat power or the mill can craft supplies so they last longer on the board. Items can easily turn the tides of a battle.

War is Coming

Kingdom of Krawall starts quite peacefully, but this does not last for long. It quickly evolves into a chaotic war for dominance where each unit, each item, and each decision matters. Can you find your way to victory?
