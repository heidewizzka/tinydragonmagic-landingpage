We are happy to present, Kingdom of Krawall, a fast-paced turn-based 4X game, where you try to conquere your enemies.

Become A King

You are the king and your desicions will decide the faith of your little kingdom.

Cards Are Your Orders

Cards representing your orders. Depending on where you use them, they have a different effect. Servants for example, can be used to claim neutral land, gather resources on your own tiles, can be trained into units in some buildings or craft items in other ones.

Conquere Them All

To be victorious, read the map, expand your realm, exploit resources and anomalies, claim important positions before rivals do to finally conquere their palaces.

Construct Buildings

Builders construct buildings. Buildings produce resources, cards and give you training and crafting options. The tile decides which building options you have.

Grow Your Economy

Each turn your kingdom produces cards and resources. A strong production, is the key to a powerful army. Buildings and servants have adjecancy bonuses and items boost both of them directly.

Anomalies and Special Buildings

Anomalies allow the construction of special buildings. Some of them give you cards, other training options or resources. Claiming them will give you an advantage and heavily influence the course of the game.

Equipe your Units

Items can be used to boost buildings or powerup your units. The blacksmith can craft armor, which gives your units more combat power or the mill can craft supplies so they last longer on the board. Items can easily turn the tides of a battle.

War is Coming

Kingdom of Krawall starts quite peaceful, but this does not last for long. Quickly it evolves into a chaotic war for dominance where each unit, each item and each decision matters. Can you find your way to victory?
