About the Game

Short Description

Kingdom of Krawall is a fast-paced 4X strategy game where players build a kingdom, use cards as commands, and fight for control of the map in solo or multiplayer matches.

Long Description

Kingdom of Krawall condenses the depth of classic 4X strategy into shorter, sharper sessions. Players expand their realm, collect resources, construct buildings, train units, craft items, and use cards to give orders. Every tile, building, unit, and item can influence the path to victory, while rival kingdoms compete for the positions and resources needed to conquer enemy palaces.
