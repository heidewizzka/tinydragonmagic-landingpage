About the Game

Short Description

Kingdom of Krawall is a fast-paced 4X strategy game with elements of a deckbuilder where players rule their kingdoms by playing cards and win a match by conquering all palaces on the map.

Long Description

Kingdom of Krawall combines the depth of a classic 4X strategy game with the immediacy of a card game, delivering sharp tactical decisions in compact, fast-paced strategic matches.

You command your kingdom by playing cards on a strategic map. Cards can be used to place units, gather resources, construct buildings, craft items, and trigger other effects. Expand your realm, build an economy, craft items, and raise an army. Your final goal is to dominate the battlefield, crush your enemies, claim their palaces, and become the King of Krawall.

Each turn, your kingdom produces new cards. Some buildings generate cards over time and you can provide food to gain more servant cards, use servants in buildings to craft item and unit cards, loot cards from neutral tiles, or raid other players and steal cards from them.

Every card can be used in multiple ways, and each decision shapes the fate of your kingdom. Victory requires more than military strength: you must balance expansion, production, economy, and timing. Master the map and your hand to conquer the Kingdom of Krawall.

Features

  • Fast-paced turn-based 4X strategy with cards on a hex map in compact sessions (45min)
  • 50+ unit and resource cards
  • 30+ item cards to equip buildings and units
  • Singleplayer with tutorial and 20+ handcrafted maps
  • Local and online PVP/Co-op multiplayer
  • Map Editor to create custom maps and share with the community
